My Favorite City

Cebu City, known as the "Queen City of the South," is the oldest city in the Philippines and a major hub of commerce, culture, and tourism. Located on the eastern coast of Cebu Island, it blends historic landmarks like Fort San Pedro and the Basilica del Santo Niño with modern developments such as IT parks and bustling ports. Its vibrant mix of heritage and progress makes it one of the country’s most dynamic urban centers.
