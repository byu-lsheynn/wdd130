Detailed Trip Descriptions

The Rapid Run (Class III)

Experience the perfect mix of excitement and scenery on our classic half-day trip! Great for beginners and families, the Rapid Run takes you through standard Class III rapids, offering thrill with intervals of calm waters.

Duration: 3 Hours | Includes: Equipment, Guide

The All-Day Adventure (Class IV)

Take on the full challenge with our Full Day Adventure! Navigating powerful Class IV rapids, this trip requires more skill and endurance but offers breathtaking canyon views and a mid-day riverside lunch. Prepare to get wet!

Duration: 6 Hours | Includes: Equipment, Guide, Lunch

Family Float (Class I-II)

Our most relaxing option, the Family Float, is designed for those seeking a tranquil scenic experience. Gentle Class I and II rapids allow you to take in the stunning wilderness surroundings. Minimum age: 5

Duration: 2 Hours | Includes: Equipment, Guide
